It has only been six weeks since the Battle of France ended.
And now a long battle has begun above Britain's skies.
However, the Luftwaffe is planning one final attack to destroy the RAF, Operation "Eagle Attack".
This offensive will start on the 13th of August 1940, codenamed "Adlertag", or "Eagle Day".
Your mission is to destroy Germany's attacking force, and manage to survive.
